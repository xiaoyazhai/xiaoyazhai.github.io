Faculty

Xiaoya Zhai

Assistant Professor, USTC School of Mathematical Sciences

Dr. Xiaoya Zhai advances high-performance, manufacturable architectures that also provide rich physical sensing and interaction, integrating materials, structures, and intelligence toward a more sustainable future. Her work has led to 40+ publications in leading journals, including Nature Communications, Advanced Science, Composite Part B, CMAME, etc.

Research Interests Topology Optimization, Isogeometric Analysis, Additive Manufacturing, Machine Learning for Design
Education Postdoc, CUHK (2023) · Ph.D., TU Delft&USTC (2021)
